## Rationale

- Each book is a full-length epic fantasy novel at roughly 105k-107k words.
- $4.99 keeps the ebooks in the common indie fantasy full-novel band while leaving room for launch promotions.
- The print prices are set for 6 x 9 in black-and-white cream interiors and should be checked against KDP's live royalty calculator before final submission.
- Use the same ebook list price on Amazon KDP, Apple Books, and Google Play Books unless running a coordinated launch promotion.

## Platform Notes

- Do not enroll the ebooks in KDP Select while Apple Books and Google Play Books editions are active.
- Set Kindle DRM to `No`, matching the prior wide-store publishing pass.
- Use KDP free ISBNs for paperback and hardcover unless owned ISBNs are assigned before the final upload.
- AI-content disclosure should be answered honestly at upload time: the manuscripts and cover art were AI-assisted and human-directed/edited.
