## Rationale

- The First Spiral is the flagship full-length novel and should be priced as a serious indie space-opera launch, but $3.99 keeps it below the common $4.99 full-length comp and improves conversion.
- Books 01-05 and 08 are short discovery reads. At $0.99, they are impulse buys and funnel readers toward the combined novel and later series entries.
- Foundations of Flame is longer than the very short entries, but still short enough that $1.99 is a better acquisition price than $2.99.
- Harmonic Rebirth is long enough to carry novel pricing, but should stay below the flagship so the combined book remains the obvious best-value purchase.
- KDP hardcover should only be published for 00, 06, and 16 in the current format plan. The generated hardcover cover files for every title use the same 0.16 in front-art left shift, but the shorter books remain Kindle/paperback editions unless the format plan changes.

## Platform Notes

- Use these same ebook prices on Amazon KDP, Apple Books, and Google Play Books unless running a temporary promotion.
- KDP ebook royalty is lower below $2.99, but the short books are priced for visibility and conversion rather than per-unit royalty.
- KDP paperback prices under $9.99 may use the lower print royalty tier. That is acceptable here because these are short, low-friction print editions.
- KDP print editions use 6 x 9 in, black-and-white interior, cream paper.
- Do not upload short-book hardcover packages to KDP even though local files exist unless the format plan changes.
