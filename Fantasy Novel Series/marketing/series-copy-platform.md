# Marketing Copy Platform

## Development Comparison

For internal use only: epic quest scale, party dynamics, and escalating set pieces inspired by fantasy games and classic secondary-world journeys.

Public-facing copy should sell the original hook: an ordinary girl hears a buried world waking and gathers enemies into a family before kings, dragons, and the dead tear the living world apart.

## Series Logline

When Mara Venn, an expendable ash-runner from a poisoned mining city, discovers she can hear the buried hearts of dead dragons, she becomes the most wanted person in Vael Taryn: a tool for kings, a threat to elves, a miracle to rebels, and the only witness who can stop ancient grief from turning the living world into an obedient afterlife.

## Short Pitch

Mara Venn was hired to crawl into places no one important could fit and no one powerful wanted to die in. Then the stone under the mines spoke her name.

Now cinders are waking across Vael Taryn, undead armies are marching under royal banners, dragons are stirring beneath mountains and seas, and every faction wants to decide what Mara is before she can decide for herself. To survive, she must gather a disgraced knight, a dark-elf exile, a light-elf oathkeeper, a gnome engineer, a goblin road-scout, a troll oath-sister, and an undead witness into a company strong enough to challenge the old covenant of the world.

## Taglines

- The world needs a chosen one. It gets the girl it tried to spend.
- Dead dragons remember. So do the people buried with them.
- She was never meant to matter. That is why the world cannot control her.
- Crowns burn the dead for power. Mara Venn hears them answer.
- The final war begins under the floor of a mining town.

## Book One Back-Cover Direction

Mara Venn has spent her life in the cinder vents beneath Kell Ashgate, small enough for the lethal work and poor enough that no one asks whether she is afraid. When a royal inspection ends with missing miners, moving corpses, and a voice under the stone calling her by name, Mara discovers the kingdom's warmth is fed by the unnamed dead.

Hunted by the crown and wanted by powers she does not understand, Mara flees into the Gloamfen with a disgraced knight, a goblin guide, and a secret that could burn cities awake. Her road will lead through troll bridges, drowned archives, and the hidden foundry beneath Harrowmere's throne.

To save her city, Mara must do the one thing the powerful never expected from someone like her: make the invisible count.

## Book Two Back-Cover Direction

Mara Venn saved a city and became a symbol. That is far more dangerous than being a fugitive.

As rebel banners rise in her name, light elves seek to sanctify her, dark elves seek to imprison her, and gnome engines trace a waking pattern beneath the continent. The road leads down into Orrowen, where dead courts still hold session and the buried moon of a dragon remembers the crime that split the elven world.

To stop a holy war, Mara must uncover the truth both sides were built to hide. But truth has weight underground, and every step toward peace wakes something older than victory.

## Book Three Back-Cover Direction

The dead rise in daylight. Dragons return as judges. Every kingdom wants Mara Venn to end the war by becoming its weapon.

At the dead capital of Vael, the first dragon dreams of a world without grief, war, or choice. Its Choir promises mercy through perfect stillness, and after all she has lost, Mara understands the temptation.

But the girl who was once treated as expendable will not save the world by deciding who else must be spent. To end the final war, Mara must free the cinders, break the covenant that made crowns stronger than people, and trust the living with the terrifying work of being free.
