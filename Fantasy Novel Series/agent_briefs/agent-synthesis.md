# Agent Synthesis

Five specialist passes shaped this foundation.

## MMO / JRPG Fantasy Consultant

Useful takeaways:

- Keep the game-like feeling under the hood: party roles, progression, trials, faction reputation, boss phases, and new access to dangerous regions.
- Do not show mechanics directly in prose.
- Every dungeon needs ecology, faction purpose, moral rule, monster hierarchy, thematic boss, and permanent consequence.
- Use downtime chapters so the party becomes beloved through repair, food, scars, jokes, arguments, and cultural friction.
- The heroine should win the finale through understanding, alliance, sacrifice, and system change, not a larger beam of magic.

Integrated into:

- Book outlines.
- Action and magic sections of the style guide.
- Creature ecology.

## Backup Fantasy Writer

Useful takeaways:

- The strongest original premise is cinders as buried dragon-hearts, mined as fuel and memory.
- Mara Venn is an ash-runner, not secret royalty.
- The emotional engine is exploitation, memory, and the fear of becoming the oppressor after gaining power.
- Book escalation should be city rebellion, continental faction war, mythic world crisis.

Integrated into:

- Series overview.
- World and magic.
- All three book outlines.

## Typesetter

Useful takeaways:

- Use one chapter per file.
- Target 400k-500k total words.
- Keep a style bible, continuity register, glossary, and chapter structure from day one.
- Avoid manual formatting, inconsistent invented names, and untracked travel/injury/item continuity.

Integrated into:

- Production plan.
- Continuity register.
- Folder structure.

## Copywriter One

Useful takeaways:

- Strong underdog framing: the world needs a chosen one and gets her instead.
- Make public-facing copy action-forward and emotional.
- Emphasize complete book arcs plus escalation.
- The party-as-family promise matters for market appeal.

Integrated into:

- Marketing platform.
- Series overview.

## Copywriter Two

Useful takeaways:

- Public positioning should not rely on comparison titles.
- Avoid crystal-chosen-warrior magic, one-object disposal silhouettes, good-light/evil-dark elf binaries, and famous fantasy naming echoes.
- The best emotional promise is an ordinary girl hearing a buried world wake and gathering enemies into a family.

Integrated into:

- IP distance rules.
- Marketing platform.
- Style guide.
