# The Cinderwake Trilogy

An original epic fantasy trilogy development workspace.

Development compass: a sweeping party adventure with the escalation, tactical set pieces, strange realms, and emotional found-family momentum of classic fantasy games and grand quest literature, without borrowing protected worlds, characters, names, plots, or signature lore.

## Core Promise

Mara Venn is an expendable ash-runner from a poisoned mining city, hired to crawl through lethal cinder vents because she is small, poor, and easy to replace. When she discovers she can hear the buried hearts of dead dragons, every power in Vael Taryn tries to use her: kings, light elves, dark elves, gnome guilds, goblin caravans, troll oath-clans, undead saints, and the few surviving dragons.

Mara's rise is not a secret-royal reveal. She becomes heroic because she keeps choosing people over power, even when power looks like the only way to save them.

## Trilogy

1. **The Ash Beneath the Crown** - Mara saves her mining city and exposes the crown's undead foundry.
2. **The Moon Below the World** - Mara stops a holy war between light and dark elves by revealing the crime both courts were built to hide.
3. **The Dragon That Dreamed Death** - Mara enters the death-dream of the first dragon and frees the world from the old covenant that made crowns stronger than people.

## Workspace

- `series_bible/` - durable world, magic, peoples, factions, cast, and continuity foundations.
- `outlines/` - book-by-book structural plans for 350-600 page novels.
- `manuscripts/` - chapter-per-file drafting space.
- `production/` - typesetting, style, continuity, and revision controls.
- `marketing/` - copy platform, blurbs, title paths, and reader promise.
- `agent_briefs/` - synthesis of the specialist-agent input used to shape the project.
