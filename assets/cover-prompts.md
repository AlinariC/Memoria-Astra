# Memoria Astra Cover Art Prompts

Use these prompts for higher-end image-model cover replacements. Generate at
4000x6000 or 3000x4500, vertical 2:3, sRGB, no text, no watermark. Keep quiet
space near the top third and bottom fifth for later typography.

## 00 - The First Spiral

Epic painterly cosmic science-fiction cover, vertical 2:3 composition, no text,
no typography, no watermark. The whole first Memoria Astra arc collapses into
one sacred emblem: Venusian gold memory, Terran pearl mist, Martian crimson,
Divide blue-violet, Lyra rose-white, and first-star gold braided into a single
opal spiral. A dark collapse seed or black-hole remnant sits low-middle, while
six subtle planetary memory stations orbit the spiral as abstract echoes rather
than separate scenes. The mood is elegiac, mythic, transcendent, and intimate:
creation remembered through grief, not explosive war. Palette of primordial
black, deep indigo, pearl mist, Venus gold, rust-crimson, Divide violet,
rose-white, and newborn star-gold.

Sales positioning: this is the definitive opening novel of the Memoria Astra
Cycle, not a simple boxed-up bundle of the short editions.
